About'SiSi.ng

'SiSi.ng is a fast growing fashion brand, that specializes in thrift and bespoke wears. We started 2018 and have excellently serviced over 500 customers in different states within Nigeria and even beyond. Our ultimate goal is ensuring at least one lady in any community, owns a SiSi wear. Join the movement, become the SiSi of your dreams.
